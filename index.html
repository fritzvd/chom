bio

Possibly best described as Indie Pop, Indie Rock Cheeses of Mexico have a history of making the crowd go wild and clearly having fun while doing it. At the end of 2016 they laid their instruments down and with it they're Folky roots. Now after a 7 year break Cheeses of Mexico returns with a new sound. A bit more rock, a bit of psychedelia, but still as energetic as ever.

You might know them from guerilla performances during the Bevrijdingsfestival in Wageningen, drenched in rain dancefests or wild squatter parties on the fringes of Amsterdam.

Cheeses of Mexico: on drums Jilles, guitar Jonathan, bass guitar Daniel, guitarist and lead vocals Fritz.

CoM are based in the Netherlands.

Contact us: cheesesofmexico at gmail dot com.
