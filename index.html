Cheeses of Mexico

bio

After a 7 year break Cheeses of Mexico returns with a new sound. A bit more rock, a bit of psychedelia, but still as energetic as ever.

You might know them from guerilla performances during the Bevrijdingsfestival in Wageningen, drenched in rain dancefests or wild squatter parties on the fringes of Amsterdam.

We sure have our own sound, but some have said we sound similar to: Moss, Wild Beasts and Deerhunter

Contact us: cheesesofmexico at gmail dot com.
